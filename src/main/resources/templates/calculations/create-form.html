UCP Calculation Formula
Main Formula:

UCP = (UUCW + UAW) × VAF

Components:
  • UUCW = Sum of use case weights
    (Unadjusted Use Case Weight)
  • UAW = Sum of actor weights
    (Unadjusted Actor Weight)
  • VAF = 0.65 + (0.01 × TDI)
    (Value Adjustment Factor)
  • TDI = Sum of all 14 technical factors' influence scores
    (Total Degree of Influence)
Effort Estimation:

Estimated time = UCP × 20 hours

The formula has been simplified by using a fixed coefficient instead of multiple levels.
