UCP Calculation Process
UCP Calculation Formula:

UCP = UUCP × TCF × ECF

Where:

  • UUCP (Unadjusted Use Case Points) = UAW + UUCW
  • UAW (Unadjusted Actor Weight) = Sum of all actor weights
  • UUCW (Unadjusted Use Case Weight) = Sum of all use case weights
  • TCF (Technical Complexity Factor) = 0.6 + (0.01 × TF)
  • ECF (Environmental Complexity Factor) = 1.4 + (-0.03 × EF)
Effort Estimation:

Estimated hours = UCP × Productivity Factor

The Productivity Factor can vary based on project characteristics:

  • 20 hours/UCP for favorable conditions
  • 32 hours/UCP for average conditions
  • 48 hours/UCP for difficult conditions
